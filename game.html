Welcome

Life can be random and unfair. With the power of hindsight, you learn which path in life is best.

Each turn you must select from one of three paths as time moves on. If you can survive until time travel is invented, you will have the chance to go back and give guidance to your past-self.

Press 'X' to close this window, then click on the circles to make life decisions.
